Club Rules & Guidelines

Rules and Regulations For Branch Ambassador

  • Branch Ambassadors will complete the tasks allocated to them in time.
  • Branch Ambassadors will not misuse, disclose, or reveal any confidential information related to Abhivyakti Club.
  • Prior permission from the concerned person in Abhivyakti Club must be taken for any planned leave (over email).
  • In case of unplanned leave, intimation should be given by text message to the concerned person.
  • Any document/material prepared by Branch Ambassadors for Abhivyakti Club during the term will be considered as the club's belongings.
  • Records of any event/session organized by Abhivyakti Club will be considered as the club's belongings.
  • Branch Ambassadors need to inform at least 14 days before about their intent to resign and complete their due assigned tasks within the given period.
  • Branch Ambassadors will obey all the instructions of their respective Section Coordinators.
  • The nominal commitment cum training fees are nonrefundable.
  • Abhivyakti Club reserves the right to terminate the candidature at any stage on the grounds of indiscipline, performance not being up to the mark, or absence without notice to the concerned person.

By becoming a Branch Ambassador, you agree to adhere to these rules and contribute positively to the Abhivyakti Club.

Rules and Regulations For Section Coordinator

  • Section Coordinators will complete the tasks allocated to them in time.
  • Section Coordinators will not misuse, disclose, or reveal any confidential information related to Abhivyakti Club.
  • Prior permission from the concerned person in Abhivyakti Club must be taken for any planned leave (over email). In case of un-planned leave, intimation should be given by text message to the concerned person.
  • Any document/material prepared by section coordinators for Abhivyakti Club tenure will be considered as the club's belongings.
  • Records of any event/session organized by Abhivyakti Club will be considered as the club's belongings.
  • Section coordinators need to inform at least 22 days before about their intent for resignation and complete their due assigned tasks within given period.
  • Performance of the team of Branch Ambassador is the responsibility of Section Coordinators.
  • Section Coordinators will obey all the instructions of their Chief Coordinator.
  • Section coordinators will receive their offer letter after the completion of their training period.
  • Abhivyakti Club reserves the right to terminate the candidature at any stage of the on following grounds:Disciplinary,Performance not being up to the mark,Absence without any advance notice to the concerned person

Our Achievements & Milestones

The Abhivyakti Club takes immense pride in the accomplishments of its members and the milestones achieved over the years. Our commitment to fostering excellent communication and leadership skills has led to numerous successes:

  • Inter-College Debate Champions: Our teams have consistently secured top positions in inter-college debate competitions across the region, showcasing superior argumentative skills and critical thinking.
  • Public Speaking Excellence: Many of our members have won accolades in various public speaking contests, including extempore, elocution, and declamation, demonstrating their ability to command an audience.
  • Successful Placement Records: Alumni who were active members of Abhivyakti Club often attribute their strong communication skills developed here to their success in securing placements in leading companies.
  • Annual RJIT Fest Contributions: We play a pivotal role in organizing and conducting communication-based events during the annual RJIT Fest, drawing large participation and positive feedback.
  • Workshop Impact: Our specialized workshops on personality development, interview skills, and group discussions have received overwhelmingly positive feedback, directly contributing to students' personal and professional growth.
  • Leadership Roles: Many of our former members have gone on to assume significant leadership roles in various student bodies and organizations within and outside the institute.
  • Community Engagement: We have organized and participated in community outreach programs, using communication as a tool for social awareness and positive change.
  • Continuous Growth: The club has seen a consistent increase in membership and engagement, reflecting its growing popularity and the value it provides to students.

These achievements are a testament to the dedication of our members and the supportive environment fostered by the Abhivyakti Club.
